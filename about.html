About Us

A.Snap LLC was founded in 2025 to meet a growing demand for quality
Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetists (CRNAs) in Colorado. Meet our expert leadership team:

Alissa Smith

Position: Founder

Alissa received her Bachelor of Science in Nursing from the University of Minnesota in 2012. After building a strong foundation working as a Pediatric Cardiovascular Intensive Care Registered Nurse and an adult Critical Care Registered Nurse, she shifted her focus to pursuing graduate studies. Alissa received a Master of Science degree in Nurse Anesthesiology from Michigan State University in 2019. Alissa now works in hospitals and surgery centers across North Denver and Boulder, Colorado. This includes Thornton, Louisville, Broomfield, Longmont, and even as far north as Fort Collins. Alissa has built a strong reputation in Colorado as a talented Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetist who is passionate about improving the patient experience.
